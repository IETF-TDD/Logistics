The Technical Deep Dive (TDD) is a venue for tutorials and presentations that provide insights into technologies which span multiple areas. TDD sessions are usually
organized during IETF weeks, but other configurations may be considered. An overview of previous TDD sessions can be found at: https://datatracker.ietf.org/group/tdd/meetings/.

In order to arrange for sessions covering various IETF areas and identify timely topics, we would like to have a team of TDD Chairs that will be responsible for
the following:

* Identify and suggest topics for future TDD sessions.
* Approach and engage with presenters to cover specific topics.
* Coordinate with presenters to build a session strawman.
* Put in place a procedure to receive unsolicited talk requests.
* Socialize TDD within the IETF and outside (RIPE, NANOG, etc.).

If you are interested to join this team, please drop us an email. Please indicate the area(s) that you can cover.

The team will be selected to ensure complementarity, diversity, and wide topics coverage.
